About Me

I started out my career (many) years ago as a marketeer, working for reknowned companies in the tourism, fitness and real estate areas.

A few years later, right after my first solo trip around the world, I travelled back in time to revive a dear passion of mine: writing. My CV now featured the additional job title of "Content Writer".

Then, all of a sudden, I discovered what Front-end Developing was all about. I was hooked. The constant challenge and excitment of seeing things working exactly like they were meant to work on the web was exactly what I had been looking for.

Nowadays, I thrive on developing websites using HTML, Javascript and CSS. And the frequent "aHA" moments that coding offers me, are what I live for.
