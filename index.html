About Me

I started out my career as a marketeer, working for renowned companies mainly in the tourism and travel areas.

A few years later, right after my first solo trip around the world, I travelled back in time to revive a dear passion of mine: writing. My CV now featured the additional job title of "Content Writer".

Then, all of a sudden, I discovered what Front-end Developing was all about. I was hooked. Nowadays, I thrive on developing websites using HTML, Javascript and CSS. And the frequent "aHA" moments that coding offers me, are what I live for.
